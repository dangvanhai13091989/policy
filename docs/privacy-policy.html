Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

MoneyNote ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance management application developed by HaiDV. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection & Storage

MoneyNote stores all your data locally on your device only. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

The data stored locally includes:

  • Transaction records (income and expenses)
  • Budget goals
  • Reminders
  • App preferences (theme, language, currency)

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Backup & Export

The app provides backup and restore functionality. When you use this feature:

  • Data is exported as a JSON file stored on your device
  • You choose where to save or share this file
  • We do not have access to your backup files

5. Children's Privacy

MoneyNote does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal data.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings > Delete All Data option. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
